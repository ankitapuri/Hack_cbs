# Marshal

Tired of applying for **_various jobs_** or **_internships_** online?Tired of trying to keep a **_track_** of all your **_applications_** and **_tasks_**?

We have come up with a **remarkable solution** of all your problems!
We give you **Marshal**.

**_Marshal_** is the act of _assembling_ and getting ready for **_action_** (especially in a war).

## What is Marshal?
**Marshal** is a  website that enables the users to keep a track of all their professiona tasks.
- It is built completely on **CSS**, **JavaScript** and **HTML**. 
- Every user can create their own **_personal account_** to track their progress.
- A **_personalized dashboard_** has been provided to the users.
- The users can *add* and *track* their **_tasks/internships_** in this dashboard.
- The users can also search for new internships or jobs using our Classifides Feature.
